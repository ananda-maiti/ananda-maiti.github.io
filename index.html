Desktop Sensor

Setup Steps

  1. Place the device on the right or left of your monitor or laptop screen.
  2. Connect the USB to the device.
  3. Sit down.
  4. Connect the USB to your laptop, CPU, monitor, or any power source. Any USB port will work.
  5. Observe the LED on the device. It will blink three times, indicating a successful initialization.
  6. Then the LED should remain off while you are seated. Keep it at a distance of about 50–70 cm from you.
  7. Adjust the top sensor so that it points over your head, while the bottom sensor points towards you.
  8. Now when you stand up, you will be blocking the top sensor and the LED should turn on.
  9. The LED should go off when you sit down and turn on when you stand up.
  10. That is it. Leave the device as it is wherever it is placed.
